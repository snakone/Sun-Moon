Rules

Pokémon will not recover HP and PP during or after battle.

Cannot use Pokémon Heal or bag items in Victory Road.

Newly caught Pokémon can be assigned to Victory Road Formation. (Before reset)

Pokémon in formation will not be affected by exterior upgrades and boosts.

Opponents of each stage will be chosen based on the power of Pokémon the player has.

Each time player clear the Victory Road, they have a chance to encounter PokeSmuggler.

VIP13 players or above will get double rewards from the boxes they pick up.

VIP5 players or above can use Quick Kill. This will complete Victory Road till stage 10.

Requeriments

There is no level restriction, but We recommend minimum Level 50.

You need to have more than 10 Pokémon around your level.

If You want to clear Victory Road everytime 100% guaranteed, it will take around 2 hours.

You must have every attack type covered. Example:

Energy Ball to kill Swampert or Kyogre

Ice Beam to kill Garchomp or Giratina

Aura Sphere to kill Tyranitar or Darkrai

Flamethrower to kill Scizor or Snorlax Y

You need Pokémon with Recover, Roost, Slack Off, etc... This will help You a lot to clear your Expedition.

Starting

Let's start the expedition to Victory Road!

What is Victory Road?

Victory Road is a serie of challenges against random real trainers, although they are controlled by CPU. There are a total of 15 stages, each one harder than the last, making the stage 15 the harder.

Each stage has a chest reward, so it's important to reach the last stage, this means more rewards!

When can I do Victory Road?

Everyday You can reset Victory Road once. It doesn't reset automatically, so even if You miss to do Victory Road one day (after 00:00 GMT+8), don't worry.

You have another entire day to clear all the stages and reset manually to do it again.

In summary, You get an extra reset everyday.

Recommened Tips

✓ The Berry will be reset after each battle.

✓ Escape won't fail unless You are trapped.

✓ The rule of unable to recover HP or PP after battle not only work for you, but also your rival.

✓ The buff or debuff will be reset after battle.

Recommened Pokémon

Tank liked Pokémon that can Recover HP.

Pokémon with high SPD and S.ATK or ATK.

Pokémon with debuff move like Sleep Powder and leech HP.

Pokémon with SPD and Destiny Bond. Make a Suicide team!

Strategy

Clear Victory Road everytime!

The objective of this strategy to clear Victory Road 100% guaranteed is to defeat every opposing Pokémon with 1 move K.O. Make " Super Effective" move every turn is a MUST.

You need to escape battle every time You can't kill the enemy Pokémon in 1 move. At lower stages, enemy Pokémon are weak and sometimes You can defeat them in 1 move K.O without making "Super Effective".

Your Pokémon can't take any damage at all till stage 10+. That's why You need to defeat the opposing Pokémon with 1 move K.O.

At this point, knowing the "Effectiveness and Weakness" table is very useful. You need to know every turn which move causes "Super Effective" to the opposing Pokémon.

Let's see how it works with a quick example:

► Put on first place a Pokémon with Grass move like Energy Ball as Mega Latias.

► You encounter a Dragonite. Your move Energy Ball is not effective against a Dragon It's time for You to press Escape button.

► Let's see back again the "Effectiveness and Weakness" table. Which move is strong against a Dragon?

The answer is Ice

► Change your Formation and put an Ice type Pokémon first. In my case I use Mega Articuno.

► Face the same Trainer again and use an Ice move against Dragonite to deal 1 move K.O.

The next opposing Pokémon is a Mega Aerodactyl. An Ice move should be enough to deal 1 move K.O, but let's think for a while.

Which type is "Super Effective" against Rock Pokémon? Exactly, Water. We have Water Pokémon. It's better if You manage moves by PP.

As You know, Ice Beam has only 10 PP, so it's better if you use another "Super Effective" move against the opposing Pokémon and save Ice Beam for stronger Pokémon You may encounter.

As You can see, I used Mega Milotic and Water Pulse to defeat Mega Aerodactyl.

You get the same 1 move K.O with the advantage that Water Pulse have way more PP than Ice Beam

► Watch this video that resume everything explained till now.

Watch the minute 4:33 of the video. Mega Lucario with Dig VS Mega Charizard X.

Maybe You are thinking why I don't used Dig that is "Super Effective" against Mega Charizard X. The answer is simply and very important.

❝You must know your Pokémon Weaknesses and Strengths and how much damage your Pokémon can deal with a specific move❞

I knew that with Dig I wouldn't be able to defeat the opponent with 1 move K.O and later Mega Lucario X would have take x2 damage from a Flamethrower.

Replacing Mega Milotic for Mega Lucario X was a good move because Mega Milotic can resist a Flamethrower very well.

Sleep Strategy

If You ever find a trainer stronger than you with more Break-up, more Level, etc... Use this strategy:

Use a Pokémon with Sleep Powder, Dark Void

When the opposing Pokémon is asleep, start to raise your stats with moves like Swords Dance or Nasty Plot

After this You will 1 move K.O every opposing Pokémon.

Happiny Strategy

If You want to heal a Pokémon and recover all your HP, use a Happiny!

First Sleep the target with a Pokémon with Sleep Powder, Dark Void...

Then change your Pokémon to Happiny, then use Healing Wish learned at Level 34.

Finally select the Pokémon You want to fully recover HP.

Easy! Let's continue the Expedition!

Stat Changes

Don't worry about Stat Changes!

► We count Stat Changes as...

Burned

Paralyzed

Sleeped

Poisoned

Frozen

Moves that changes Pokémon stats like Swords Dance or moves that lower your stats like Metal Sound.

When any of your Pokémon get one of this stats changes, simply Escape the battle and the stat change will disappear.

► If You are in battle and the opposing Pokémon use a move to boost any of his stats, it's your opportunity to recover all your HP.

Example: In this battle Mega Mawile use Swords Dance on the first turn. This is a free turn for Us, so let's use Recover

► You can repeat this everytime You want with any of your Pokémon. Use this to recover all your Pokémon with heal move.

► Don't worry, on the first turn Mega Mawile will always use the move Swords Dance, unless You put a Pokémon weak to Ice - Dark

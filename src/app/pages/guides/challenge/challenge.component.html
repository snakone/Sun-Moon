Requeriments

  • There is no level restriction, but We recommend minimum Level 30.
  • We recommend to have more than 6 Pokémon around your level.
  • You can challenge Trainers all the times You want as long as You have Energy.
  • Challenge a Trainer cost 6 Energy while challenge Elite Trainer cost 12 Energy.
  • To RAID Trainers You need to already have been defeated the Trainer with 3 Stars.

Starting

Let's start Challenge Trainers!

What is Challenge Trainers?

While You complete the Main Quest Story Line, Yoy will face many Trainers. They are looking for a Pokémon Challenge! Once You defeat a Trainer, You will be able to challenge that Trainer back again anywhere, anytime! Make 3 Stars to challenge (RAID) a Trainer many times in a row! This will increase your rewards!

When can I Challenge Trainers?

Anytime, You just need Energy. You need to defeat a Trainer once if You want to challenge him again.

► Anytime is a good time to challenge Trainers!. You might think spending some Crystals to purchase Energy.

Recommened Tips
  • Equip Berry to your Pokémon team before challenge any Trainer.
  • Watch the opposing team before any challenge and Counter your enemy!
  • Do not mislead the opposing Pokémon Stats. Enemy Pokémon are more weaker than you think.
  • Use RAID x 10 function if you seek fast rewards. You will challenge any Trainer 10 times at once.

Elite Trainers

Harder Challenges await You!

Elite Trainers are the same as casual Trainers with the difference that they give better Rewards at expenses of costing 12 Energy. Elite Trainers will have stronger Pokémon than a normal Trainer.

► You can check which Trainers are Elites on the selection window:

Differences between Casual and Elite Trainers

  • Elite Trainers gives "Pokémon Coins" as reward, while Casual Trainers gives "Card Shards"
  • Challenge a Elite Trainer cost 12 Energy while challenge a Casual Trainer cost 6 Energy.
  • You can challenge Elite Trainer 3 times a day for free. VIP players have more chances to get more challenges.
  • There's a chance you can get Awakening Stones while fighting Elite Trainers.

Starting

Warning! Legendary Pokémon invading!

What is Pokémon Invasion?

You have the chance to get Legendary Pokémon for free.

► All You need to do is defeat a Wild Legendary Pokémon to get "Legendary Shards", with 50 You can synthesis one Legendary Pokémon.

When can I do Pokémon Invasión?

Everyday You have the chance to encounter Wild Legendary Pokémon with the following schedule:

  • 12:00 - 13:00 GMT +8
  • 15:00 - 16:00 GMT +8
  • 18:00 - 19:00 GMT +8
  • 21:00 - 22:00 GMT +8

Dont miss out! They are invading every 3 hours.

Recommened Tips
  • You can synthesis 4 different Legendary Pokémon each week.
  • Collect Legendary Shards from the same Pokémon (50 shards) if You want to synthesis 4 Pokémon each week.
  • Use Pokémon with the following moves: Wrap, Toxic, Leech Seed
  • You can defeat every Legendary Pokémon but note that weekly limit for shards is 100 only.
Recommened Pokémon
  • Pokémon with porcentual damage like Darkrai with Nightmare or Milotic with Wrap
  • Pokémon with moves like Leech Seed, Toxic or any move that can leave a Burn.
  • We also recommend Ice, Dragon and Dark Pokémon, since many Legendary Pokémon are Dragon and Psychic
Restrictions
  • Weekly limit reset at Monday 00:00 GMT +8
  • You can collect a total of 200 shards a week.
  • When You reach the Shard Limit, You still be able to defeat Legendary Pokémon but without any reward.
  • There are 2 types of shard. Type A, from A grade Legendary Pokémon. Type S, from S grade Legendary Pokémon. Each one have shard limit of 100.

Defeating Legendary Pokémon

Open the Pokémon Invasion Window and select from the list the Legendary Pokémon You want to defeat in order to get the shards.

You will auto-route till encounter the Wild Pokémon.

Each Legendary Pokémon has a different level depending on the grade and region. The futher You go, the higher Pokémon level.

Rewards

Synthesis Legendary Pokémon

Everytime You defeat a Wild Legendary Pokémon, You Will get Legendary Pokémon Shards.

5 Shards, each defeated Legendary Pokémon. Total 100 Shards per type.

Collect 50 Legendary Shards from a same Pokémon to synthesis It. You can do this on the Research Menu, Synthesis.

What can I do with Legendary Shards?

You can use 50 Legendary Pokémon Shards to synthesis a Legendary Pokémon.

You can exchange Shards for another Legendary Pokémon Shards of your selection in amount of 4:1.

First select "Research" on the main menu. Then select "Synthesis".

Search for the Pokémon You have shards for, in my case I have 60 Riolu Shards. Then select "Synthesis".

Finally, catch the Legendary Pokémon!

Exchange Legendary Shards

Use 4 Legendary Pokémon Shards of the same Pokémon and exchange them to 1 Legendary Pokémon Shard of your choice.

Let's go back to Synthesis Window... Search the Pokémon you want shards for and select "Exchange Shard".

You will see this window now:

In this case We are looking for Little Deoxys Shard. Can only exchange 4 Random Shards of same quality.

We select any Legendary Shard from the right with at least 4 of them.

After this You can Exchange All or just Exchange 1. Note that this process cost Money.

Select "Exchange" if You want to exchange just 1 Shard.

Select "Exchange All" if You want to get 2 Shards instead. Simple Math: 8/4 = 2.
